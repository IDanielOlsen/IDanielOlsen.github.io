Scenarios

#1 What makes WeatherFacts different from other weather applications?

It's simple and to the point. Colors represent the kind of weather, making it easy to tell at a glance.

#2 What devices can I use WeatherFacts on?

You can use is on any device that has browsing capabilities. Laptop, desktop, smartphone, and even some watches.

#3 Will WeatherFacts work on an unreliable mobile connection?

Of course! With simplicity in mind, we also take pride in its optimization over slower networks.

#4 Can WeatherFacts show me weather information in other places?

Currently, WeatherFacts only supports the following cities in Idaho: Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven

#5 Will there be any forms of monitization on the site?

We don't have any plans on making money on this site, so don't worry about any subscription fees, or ads ruining your experience. If you wish to donate for maintenance, contact us for more details.
